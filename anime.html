The Anime

So you want to learn about how Yu-Gi-Oh! works in the Anime then? Well, no time like the present to rip off the bandaid, the Anime, at least the original, is not only completely inaccurate to the competitive gameplay that was taking place in the TCG at the same time, it also breaks just about 90% of the basic rules of the game. From the banal, to the broken cards, to the bizzare, the Anime is essentially in its own world when it comes to the card game.
